grml-feedback(1)
================
:doctype: manpage


NAME
----
grml-feedback - give feedback to GRML


SYNOPSIS
--------
*grml-feedback* ['OPTIONS']


DESCRIPTION
-----------
The grml-feedback(8) opens a browser with a website where you can easily
provide some ideas and suggestions. All feedback is welcome, feel free
to comment in any case!


OPTIONS
-------

*-u URL*::
    Specify the URL to the feedback website, defaults to
    http://feedback.grml.org/submit-feedback.
